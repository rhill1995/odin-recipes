Lasagna

This homemade lasagne is a hearty classic, layered with ground beef, meat sauce, cheese and pasta

It is perfect for family dinners, solo meals to meal prep for the week

Ingredients

    500g Minced beef 1 Chopped finely diced 2 Garlic finely diced/minced 1x400g Tinned tomato 12 Lasagne sheets 250 ricotta cheese 200g Grated mozzarella 50g Grated Cheddar cheese Salt and/or pepper to taste Fresh basil or parsely to top (Optional)
