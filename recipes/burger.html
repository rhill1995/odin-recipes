Burger

This is the recipe for a good old classic beef burger

Suitable to eat alone or scale up the recipe to feed the family

Recipe

    1 Large egg 1/2 Teaspoon salt 1/2 Teaspoon ground black pepper 500g Minced beef 1/2 Cup fine dry bread crumbs

Cooking instructions

    Preheat oven/grill for high heat and lightly oil Whisk egg, salt and pepper together in a meduim bowl Add minced beef and bread crumbs, mix with your hands or a fork until well blended Form into 3/4-inch thick brugers Place burgers on to the preheated oven/grill and cook for 6 to 8 minutes per side
