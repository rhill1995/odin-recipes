Burger

This is the recipe for a good old classic beef burger

Suitable to eat alone or scale up the recipe to feed the family

Recipe

  • 1 Large egg
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 500g Minced beef
  • 1/2 Cup fine dry bread crumbs

Cooking instructions

  1. Preheat oven/grill for high heat and lightly oil
  2. Whisk egg, salt and pepper together in a meduim bowl
  3. Add minced beef and bread crumbs, mix with your hands or a fork until well blended
  4. Form into 3/4-inch thick brugers
  5. Place burgers on to the preheated oven/grill and cook for 6 to 8 minutes per side
